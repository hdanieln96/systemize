# Privacy Policy for Systemize

**Last updated: November 13, 2024**

## Introduction

Systemize ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

Systemize stores all user data locally on your device using SQLite database. We collect and store:

- **Tasks**: Task titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, and completion status
- **Habits**: Habit names, descriptions, frequencies, and tracking data
- **Timeline Events**: Scheduled events and their details
- **User Preferences**: App settings and customization options

### Data We DO NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or camera
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share any information with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

All your data is stored exclusively on your device and is used solely to:

- Display your tasks, habits, and timeline
- Provide productivity tracking features
- Maintain your app preferences
- Enable local notifications (if you enable them)

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
- Your data never leaves your device
- We do not have access to your data
- Data is only accessible through the app on your device
- If you delete the app, all data will be permanently deleted

## Push Notifications

If you enable notifications:
- We use Apple Push Notification service (APNs) for local notifications only
- Notifications are generated locally on your device
- We do not send remote push notifications
- No notification data is sent to our servers

## Children's Privacy

Systemize does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect any information, the app is safe for all ages.

## Third-Party Services

Systemize does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your data is accessible within the app
- **Delete**: You can delete individual items or all data by deleting the app
- **Export**: Currently, data export is not available (future feature)
- **Control**: You control all settings and preferences

## Data Backup

- We do not provide cloud backup services
- Your data is not synchronized across devices
- If you uninstall the app, all data will be lost
- We recommend using device backup solutions (iCloud backup) if available

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email**: nguyen.daniel96@icloud.com
- **App Store**: Through the app's support page

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- Apple App Store Review Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

## Summary

**In short**: Systemize is a privacy-first productivity app. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any of your information. Your productivity data is yours and yours alone.
