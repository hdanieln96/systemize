# Privacy Policy for Systemize

**Last Updated: January 12, 2025**

## Overview

Systemize ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Systemize is a local-first application. All your data including:
- Daily schedules and tasks
- Habit tracking information
- Todo lists
- Personal preferences and settings

...is stored **exclusively on your device** using local SQLite database storage.

## No Account Required

Systemize does not require:
- User accounts
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Sign-ups or registrations

You can use the app completely anonymously.

## No Third-Party Services

We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- Third-party tracking tools

## Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using:
- **SQLite database** for task and habit storage
- **iOS local storage** for app preferences

Your data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have servers)
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Permissions

Systemize may request the following device permissions:
- **Notifications** - To remind you of scheduled tasks and habits (optional)
- **Local Storage** - To save your tasks and habits on your device

We do not request access to:
- Camera
- Photo Library
- Contacts
- Location
- Microphone

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID) protects your data
- We recommend keeping your device secure
- Enable device encryption for additional security

## Children's Privacy

Systemize does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by:
1. Uninstalling the app, OR
2. Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings (if available)

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data:
- There is no data for us to access, export, or delete
- You have complete control over your data on your device
- You can delete the app and all data at any time
